About

Computational materials researcher with a PhD in Chemistry and specialist expertise in machine-learned interatomic potentials, density functional theory and high-performance computing.

Currently a Daphne Jackson Trust Fellow, developing neural network models to accelerate molecular dynamics simulations of magnesium-based hydrides, supporting the development of novel green-energy materials.

Experienced in Python, PyTorch, workflow automation, HPC, electronic structure methods, with a strong publication record and experience in collaborative research.

Research Interests

Machine-learned interatomic potentials • Atomistic modelling of solids • Hydrogen storage materials • Molecular simulation
